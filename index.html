    For Parents

    Why first principles?

    It slows the rush

    The app avoids handing over final numerical answers. It teaches the smallest ideas underneath the work, then invites the child to finish with confidence.

    It supports conversation

    Use the “Think About” questions as prompts. Ask your child to explain one building block back to you in their own words.

    It is local-first

    This demo runs in the browser. Camera access stays in the page. Real AI can be added later through a privacy-aware backend.

    It is age-flexible

    You can tune tone, detail, and examples for a young child, middle schooler, or older student by changing the demo data or future AI prompt.
